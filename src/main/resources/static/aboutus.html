About Us

Mesi Store is a convenient online drug store and specialized help system for automated search of rare and common drugs online.

Using the services of our company, you can buy in Bishkek, wholesale and retail products for health and beauty, as well as medicines at the lowest prices.

An intuitive search system allows you to find the desired trade name, find out its rating in the reference, specify how much it costs and is sold without a prescription, in a matter of seconds. If you need to bring the medicine urgently, you can use the city express delivery service, which provides for delivery of the order within three hours (at night the service can also be ordered).

Internet pharmacy Medi Store has its own large warehouse, which is a guarantee of storage of synthetic and homeopathic medicines in accordance with all the requirements and standards established by the manufacturer. By placing an order in a pharmacy, you can forget about the tedious search for drugs in the city, when you are sick, feel bad or simply do not want to leave the house - couriers of our central city network will become your reliable assistants.
